Kazimir Malevich
1878-1935

He was a Russian painter and art theoretician.
He was a pioneer of geometric abstract art and the originator of the avant-garde Suprematist movement.
He was a devout Christian mystic who believed the central task of an artist was that of rendering spiritual feeling.

"Shouldn't it give us pause that the oldest works of art are as impressive today in their beauty and spontaneity as they were many thousands of years ago?" (Kasimir Malevich)
