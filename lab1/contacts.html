Museums

You can see works of Kazimir Malevich in different museums all over the world:

  • Great Britain, London, Tate Gallery
  • USA, New-Haven, Yale University Art Gallery
  • USA, New-York, Museum of Modern Art
  • France, Paris, National Museum of Modern Art
  • Netherlands, Amsterdam, National Museum
  • Russia, Moscow, Tretyakov Gallery
  • Russia, St. Petersburg, National Russian Museum
  • Russia, Saratov, National Museum of Arts
